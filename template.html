How the strategy works

Six rules stacked in order. Each was tested in isolation; the gate ablation and the daily exit are the two single biggest contributors to the v3 numbers.

1Universe
25 USDT pairs on Binance. A coin is investable only when it has ≥90d of history AND trailing 30d ADV ≥ $25M. Failed coins (LUNA, FTT) are in the candidate pool — no survivor bias.
2Breadth gate
% of investable universe above 50d MA, mapped to tiered gross exposure (0/30/60/100%). Tier graduation is decorative; the gate itself adds +0.25 Sharpe, +30pp MaxDD.
3Sizing
Composite momentum score over 21d, 63d, 126d risk-adjusted returns. Hold top-3 equal-weight when the gate is on.
4Trend entry filter
A coin can only enter the top-N rank if close > own 50d MA AND the MA itself is rising. Strict — kills head-fakes.
5Trend exit (daily)
Asymmetric: close < own 50d MA triggers a forced sell at the next close (1-bar lag). Looser than entry so we do not whipsaw on normal pullbacks.
6Cadence and fees
Weekly Monday rebalance plus daily exit overrides. Signal at close T, traded close T+1. 10 bps per side (Binance VIP-0 spot).

What this strategy is not

Not a strict BTC-improvement

Underperforms BTC in pure BTC-led bull years (2020, 2023, 2024). Outperforms in bears (2018, 2022) and breakout altcoin years (2019, 2021). It is a different risk profile, not a better one in every regime.

Currently in a real weak patch

Anyone deploying since 2024-01-01 would have a 1.4-year Sharpe of −0.48 versus 60/40's −0.17. The strategy has underperformed for ~18 months as of writing. This is a live issue, not a backtest artefact.

2018 bear is brutal

−43% CAGR in 2018 because the rolling liquidity universe had only 3 coins investable that year (BTC, BNB, ETH) and the strict trend filter whipsawed on false bounces. Known structural wound — candidate v4 change.

What was rejected during research

Volatility-target overlay. IS Sharpe improved (0.97 vs 0.82) on the IS-best config. OOS Sharpe degraded (1.05 vs 1.20). The relationship between IS and OOS was inverted across the parameter grid. Honest IS-only protocol rejected the overlay. See scripts/research/vol_target_search.py.

Pipeline. Strategy code: scripts/backtest.py. Build: scripts/pipeline.py. Data: daily Binance USDT spot OHLCV via CCXT.

Hygiene. Signals lagged 1 bar (observed close T, traded close T+1). Fees 10 bps per side applied to every weight change. Rolling-liquidity investability mask removes survivorship bias. IS/OOS split at 2020-12-31, no peeking when parameters were chosen.

Backtest disclosure. Walk-forward validated by year-by-year forward Sharpe and block-bootstrap CI on the full sample. Single hold-out cut, not full expand-window walk-forward with re-fitting. Parameter sensitivity tested OAT on IS only.
