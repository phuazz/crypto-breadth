How the strategy works

Universe
25 USDT pairs on Binance. Rolling liquidity gate: a coin is investable only if it has ≥90d history AND trailing 30d ADV ≥ $25M. Removes survivorship bias of any fixed list.
Breadth gate
% of investable universe above 50d MA. Tiered exposure (0/30/60/100%). Gate ablation: +0.25 Sharpe, +30pp MaxDD improvement.
Sizing
Composite momentum score (21d / 63d / 126d, risk-adjusted). Hold top-3 equal-weight when on.
Trend entry filter
A coin can only enter the rank if close > own 50d MA AND MA rising. Strict — avoids head-fakes.
Trend exit (daily)
Close < own 50d MA forces a sell at the next close (1-bar lag). Looser than entry — avoids whipsaw.
Cadence and fees
Weekly Monday rebalance + daily exit overrides. Signal at close T, traded close T+1. 10 bps per side.

Regime breakdown

The strategy wins in bears and breakout years, lags BTC in pure BTC-led bull years (2020, 2023, 2024). It is a different risk profile, not a strict BTC improvement.

Per-coin contribution to gross PnL

Dollar contribution per coin = sum over time of (yesterday's weight × today's return × yesterday's equity). Sum across coins = total gross PnL before fees.

What this strategy is not

Not a strict BTC-improvement

The strategy underperforms BTC in pure BTC-led bull years (2020, 2023, 2024). It outperforms in bears (2018, 2022) and in breakout altcoin years (2019, 2021). It is a different risk profile, not a better one in every regime.

Currently in a real weak patch

Anyone deploying since 2024-01-01 would have a 1.4-year Sharpe of −0.48 versus 60/40's −0.17. The strategy has underperformed for ~18 months as of writing. This is a known live issue, not a backtest artifact.

The 2018 bear is brutal

−43% CAGR in 2018 because the rolling liquidity universe had only 3 coins investable that year (BTC, BNB, ETH) and the strict trend filter whipsawed on false bounces. Known structural wound — fix is a candidate v4 change.

What was rejected during research

Volatility-target overlay. IS Sharpe improved (0.97 vs 0.82) on the IS-best config. OOS Sharpe degraded (1.05 vs 1.20). The relationship between IS and OOS was inverted across the parameter grid — IS-best configs were OOS-worst. Honest IS-only protocol rejected the overlay. See scripts/research/vol_target_search.py.
